Extra Core Advice

Ride Responsibly Guidelines

Observe standard commuter compliance laws to preserve a safe, clean neighborhood tracking system.

A

Stick to Designated Transit Lanes: Operate e-scooters inside municipal bicycle tracks or public roads. Avoid sidewalk pedestrian spaces completely.

B

Single-Rider Restrictions: Fleet hardware structures are optimized for a single individual. Dual-passenger riding compromises steering safety metrics.

C

Observe Geofenced Parking Docks: Always secure your rental setup inside marked system return zones to eliminate public pathway obstruction lines.
